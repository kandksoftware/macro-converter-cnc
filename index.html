Macro Converter CNC
a CNC macro converter

Macro Converter CNC - is a small, handy software to convert CNC programs written with a CNC macro (based on Fanuc Macro B) into plain G-code that can be used on a CNC milling / lathe or machining center that does not use macros or for programs understandable to other operators who are not familiar with macros.
